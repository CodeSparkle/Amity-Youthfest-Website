Amity Youth Fest

CHROMA 2k18 is Amity's Youth Festival which is a 2-day annual festival organised by the Amity University Madhya Pradesh, Gwalior. It is an annual gathering of students from amity university, other amity universities and other universities & colleges. The objective of Amity Youth Festival (CHROMA) is living up to their old-age reputation of coolness blended with intellect. CHROMA makes sure the city comes to a stand still and all roads lead towards Amity University when CHROMA commences. From professional shows to amateur bands, from Classical dance to Choreography, from competitive events to informal events, the festival has it all. With 6000+ participating students, CHROMA 2018 guarantees to be a sure shot hit.
This brochure will give you the complete insight of the events and how can you associate with us. "You haven’t seen life fella, if you haven’t seen this fest"
