SPIN A TALE

Stories allow us to imagine, visualize and get inspired by what does not exist, most of the times. It is a means to convey a thought, information, or simply, one’s own feelings about a particular thing, event or person.
MODUS OPERANDI
1. Participation mode – Single or Team (2 participants)
2. Eligibility
a. The Competition is opened to school students (6 to 12) and also to College and University students.
b. The students have to register themselves either Online (Amity University’s Website) or Offline (Forms) for registration.
3. Single Round
RULES
1. The story should be original.
2. The sequence of the participants would be decided by Draw of Lots.
3. Each participant/team would be asked to narrate a story with inclusion of a phrase which would be given to them.
4. The Time limit would be 5 minutes.
5. Both English and Hindi could be used as a Medium for delivering the story.
6. The participants would be judged on the basis of
• Language Proficiency,
• Presentation/Delivery
• Theme/Outline.
7. The decision of the Judges would be last and final.

DUMPSTER

Prove that the 3R’s – reuse, reduce and recycle are the best strategies to make the most out of waste materials. Even waste can be useful, productive or can be turned into a decorative piece…let your imagination flow free..!! Get your stuff to be the best. Promote sustainable development in your own way, reuse today, for a new tomorrow.
Use your skills to create the best useful materials from scratch. Become an engineer, craftsman, artist, architect or a dumpster driver on this exciting race to win the PRIZE.
RULES
1) Raw materials will be provided to the participants (except scissors, fevicol, tape, colours, brush, pins and other basic stationary material).
2) Each entry can have a maximum of 3 people in the group.
3) Time limit is 2 hrs.
4) Participants will be judged on the basis of creativity, neatness, complexity level and the use of non-degradable materials.
5) Use of Mobile phones and other electronics devices are not allowed.

COSPLAY

EVENT DESCRIPTION
This is a costume display contest in which participants will imitate any one of their favorite character from a film, book or game by their unique attire and make up.
RULES
1. Team of three members (2 designers and one model) can participate.
2. Participants have to recreate the chosen look within the given time period on the spot.
3. Participants have to do a catwalk and deliver one of their signature dialogue or movement at the end.
4. Participants must carry their college I.D.
5. They must get ready on the spot.
6. Only one hour will be given for costume and make-up and last thirty minutes will be for execution and evaluation.
7. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA
• Imitation skills
• Catwalk
• Dialogue delivery/signature move

DRESS ME UP

EVENT DESCRIPTION
• This is a creative designing and color rendering competition.
• Participants will be provided with basic female or male sketch, drawing sheets
• They have to dress up either a male or female figure choosing one out of the following given themes.
• They have to use a combination of different media (e.g. tissue paper, onion skins, flower petals, pencil waste, plastics etc) along with colors and sequins to create a 3-D effect.
• They have to give a brief explanation about their work at the end.
THEMES
1. Tech art (e.g wires, cables, damaged electronic parts, nails and bolts and other small technical items, decorative items)
2. Cotton candy (e.g raw cotton, colored cotton balls, cotton threads, laces etc.)
*Fabric swatches are not allowed
3. Blossoms (e.g fresh flowers, dried flowers, leaves, twigs and other organic plant parts)
4. Build from ruins (e.g waste papers, candy covers, burnt matchsticks, waste fabric swatches etc.)
RULES
1. Participants must carry their college I.D.
2. Participants must bring their own supplies and art materials.
3. They have to design and render on the spot.
4. They can choose only one theme.
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA
1. Innovativeness
2. Presentation
3. Neatness
4. Adherence to the theme
5. Explanation

BEE WITCHED

Spelling Bee is long-standing convention that promotes spelling skills and vocabulary of students and fosters their deportment for public speaking. The objective of organizing this competition is to improve and advance the skills associated with good spelling habits, to expand students’ vocabulary and to encourage accurate word usage and pronunciation. The excitement of learning spellings, new words, their meaning and origin, and application is the most significant feature of this championship.
ELIGIBILITY FOR PARTICIPATION
This spelling championship is open to all school and university students. Entries will be accepted in teams comprising maximum 03 participants each.
PHASES OF THE CONTEST
1. MCQ Test
2. Jumbled words
3. Audio Round
4. Visual Round
5. Rapid Fire
