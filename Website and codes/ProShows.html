BEAT THE STREET

RULES
1) Registration fee is to be paid before the competition.
2) Participant should bring their receipt of the registration.
3) Student/Participant should bring their Original College/School ID.
4) Styles: Hip-Hop, Popping, Tutting, krumping, Break-dance, Stepping, B-boying, Contraptions and Western Freestyle. Deviation from hip-hop is discouraged.
5) The Street Dance will comprise of two rounds.
ROUND 1
i. Participants are expected to prepare themselves for face-off, as no preparation time will be given.
ii. In each round a member shall enter the battle area and perform their set or throw down for a fixed period of time. Once their time is over they will be informed to move from the battle and let their opponents to enter the battle and perform their set.
iii. The decisions of the judges will be final and binding.
ROUND 2
i. Participants should perform a dance sequence.
ii. The Retro song should be an original Hindi Bollywood song; no remix or remakes are allowed.
6) Time limit will be 3-4 minutes (+ 1min. Setup time).
7) Judging Criteria: - Choreography, energy, space utilization, costumes, innovation, overall impact, use of retro song in the performance.
VENUE
Basketball Cage, Sports Complex, AUMP

SWARANJALI

DESCRIPTION
Swaranjali is a solo singing (Hindi /Eng) event. All the events involves singing with karaoke (music without vocals) playing in background. For participation candidates need to register themselves and appear in auditions (with karaoke) and candidates of other institutes who cannot appear in auditions can send their singing videos over Whatsapp as well.
RULES
1. Registration and Audition is mandatory for all.
2. Every candidate will be given 3 minutes for performance and they need to prepare their karaoke as per the time limit.
3. Candidates need to bring and submit karaoke tracks prior to the event.
4. Decision of the judges will be final.
5. No song should contain explicit or vulgar language.
6. All candidates need to carry ID Cards of their respective institutes during registration.
7. Audition date is 29 Jan, 2018 (Monday), 12:15 pm onwards
