RULES
1. Game Version: Need For Speed Most Wanted
2. The game has to be played only on keyboard. Any other controllers are not permitted.
3. Tournament Format: 4 Players play at a time. The first two players to complete the race will advance to the next round (no. of players who advance to the next round are subject to total number of registrations for the game).
4. Game Type: Circuit \ Sprint \ Speed Trap \Lap Knock-out (to be decided based on the stage of the tournament)
5. Winning a race:
a. Sprint/Circuit: Winner is the person who finishes the race first.
b. Speed Trap/Lap Knock Out: Winner is the person who finishes first or covers the most distance in case no one finishes the race.
6. At the end of each match, players must maintain the final screens and receive confirmation from the referee. Wining rules are subject to modification by organizing committee’s own discretion.

RULES
1. No. of Rounds in the event will be subjected to registration.
2. Game will follow simple knock-out method of elimination till the end.
3. Qualifying matches will have a 4 minute half. I.e. each qualifier round match will be of 8 minutes only. NO extra time will be allotted.

COUNTER STRIKE

RULES
1. Round : 1 minute 45 second cycle on a map
2. Half : 6 rounds of play on the defending or offensive side
3. Half Time: The period between halves in which teams swap sides.
4. Match : First team to win 7 total rounds, or the single overtime round
5. Overtime: The case of a 6-6 score at the end of regulation play, 3 overtime rounds will be played. The start money for each team will be set at $10,000 per player for overtime.
6. Players : 10 (5 on each team)
7. Rounds: 12 rounds for regulation play
8. Max Rounds Format: 6 rounds as Offense and 6 rounds as Defense per team until the victory condition are met.
9. Victory Conditions: The first team to win 6 rounds in regulation or the team that wins the overtime period.
10. Buy Time: 15 Seconds
11. Start Money: $800 for regulation play, $10,000 for overtime periods
12. Freeze Time: 15 Seconds
13. C4 Timer: 35 Seconds

SIDE SELECTION
1. By knife round or coin toss (at admin discretion).
2. At half time, teams will switch sides.
3. Half time will be a maximum of two minutes, at which point both teams must be ready.
4. In the event of overtime, teams will switch sides and play an additional 6 rounds (3 on each side) with $10,000 start money; if the score is still tied, another overtime period will be played to determine the winner.
SETUP AND CONFIGURATION:
1. Non-standard game settings or third party applications that affect game play are not permitted. Players found using either will be penalized at the administrators´ discretion.
2. Crouch hopping will not be allowed.
TOURNAMENT GUIDELINES:
1. Any disconnection issues that cannot be resolved by the players in the match must be brought to the attention of an admin immediately.
2. Any use of game exploits, cheats, or any other play deemed “unfair” by an admin may result in disciplinary action up to and including disqualification from the tournament.
3. All players must save game replays and end-of-game score screen shots.
VIOLATIONS
1. Use of any unapproved textures or models - Only default CS 1.6 models will be allowed
2. Silent or unreachable bomb plants – The bomb must be audible and planted in a position that is accessible without a boost.
3. Punishment for any violation will be at the sole discretion of the administrators.
RULE CHANGES
The Administrators reserve the right to modify the rules as needed. This includes changes due to software updates or releases, event organizer decisions and all other changes deemed necessary to run a successful tournament. Teams are responsible to check the rules on a regular basis and prior to the event to ensure they are in complete compliance. Teams must understand that rules listed are guidelines to ensure fair and competitive play and are subject to interpretation by the administrators based on the spirit of the game.
