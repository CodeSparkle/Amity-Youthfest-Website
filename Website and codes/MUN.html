MODEL UNITED NATION (MUN)



Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. This activity takes place at MUN conferences.

Model United Nations helps the participants to hone their skills in researching, public speaking, debating, and writing, as well as critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership.
