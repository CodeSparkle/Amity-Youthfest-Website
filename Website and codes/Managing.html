MIND GAMES

RULES
1. This event will have three rounds, each round will have elimination.
a. Round one: dhoondte reh jaoge!!!
i. All the teams will be given some items to collect within 15 minutes.
ii. Team which brings more items within 15 minutes will qualify.
b. Round two: Bhujho to Jane!!!
i. One member of the team will pick a chit from the bowl and will tell about the word written on chit by acting (without telling any word) and other members will give the answer.
ii. 2 minutes will be given to each team to judge the answer; rank of the team will be done on the basis of time taken to answer.
c. Round Three: Word Power
i. Every team will be given 8 letters and 2 minutes. The team needs to prepare as many words possible within 2 minutes.
ii. Marks will be given on the basis of number of words forms by each team and number of letters in each word.
2. After round Three two teams will be selected as winner (1st and 2nd) and will be awarded with some cash prize.

JUST A MINUTE

DESCRIPTION
It is a one minute game where participant will be required to perform some activities in a minute on individual basis. Just a Minute uses the simple mechanisms of physics, gravity, geometry, and other simple sciences and math in order to live up to the challenge.
The objective of these games is to identify the performance of repetitive task in a minute.
This will have short fun loving and accessible games that will require high energy levels to perform task in a minute.
RULES
1. The task is to be performed individually. 2. Basic materials to perform the task will be provided to the participants.
3. One minute time will be given to perform the task
4. Participant completing the task maximum times will go to the next level of Activity.
5. The winner is the person with the maximum number of points at the end of the round.
