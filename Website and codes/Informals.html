THE MIND BUSTER SERIES

INTRODUCTION
1) Each participant has to appear for the PRELIMS round individually.
2) It will be held in two parts:
PRELIMS
• It is mandatory for all the teams to take the prelims and the selection for the finalist will be based on their Prelim’s performance.
• The duration of the Prelim’s is 15 minutes.
• In case of a tie breaker in prelims, the clashing teams will be called for a short 5 question rapid fire round. Top 8 SCORERS will qualify for the finals.
• Finalists will be clubbed in a pair of two, forming four teams.
• Any unfair means or misconduct by the participants will lead (the whole team) to disqualification. Usage of mobile phones while any of the rounds are in progress will lead to disqualification.
FINAL
AUDIO VISUAL
i. Each team will have to answer 2 direct questions.
ii. Answering a direct question will fetch the team 10 points.
iii. Teams are allowed to pass their questions.
iv. Answering the Pass question will fetch 5 points.
v. If a question is once passed, it cannot be further passed.
vi. There is no negative marking.
RAPID FIRE
i. Each team will be asked a series of questions in rapid succession for 1 minute ONLY.
ii. There is no negative marking.
iii. Each team must try their best to answer as many questions as possible.
BUZZER ROUND
i. This is the Final Round of the competition.
ii. All teams must patiently listen to the question and press the buzzer ONLY after hearing the question. If any team presses the buzzer before the question is fully read, that team HAS to answer the question asked.
iii. There is negative marking in this round.
iv. Deduction of 10 marks will be done in case a team answers incorrectly.
v. Answering the question correctly will fetch the team 20 marks. Answering the question incorrectly will deduct 10 marks from the team’s total score.
NOTE: - Quizmaster's decision will be final.

SAMVAAD

RULES
• Participation mode – Single or Team (2 participants)
• Eligibility –
o The Competition is opened to school students (10, 11 and 12) and also to College and University students.
o The students have to register themselves either Online or Offline (Forms) for registration.
ROUND – 1 (5 minutes per participant/team)
1. The choice of language should be done in advance, and the language cannot be switched in between.
2. The first round is the opening part in which each team will send its one representative to give their view on the topic, either for or against the motion.
3. The Time limit would be 5 minutes for each speaker (for the first round) warning bell will ring after 4 minutes. Screening by the judges will be done after the first round; qualified teams will proceed for the second round.
ROUND – 2 (5+2=7 minutes per participant/team)
1. In the second round the topics will be SAME and both members of the team will speak for 5 min in total. (In the case of individual participant the time limit will be the same of 5min)
2. The speaker/speakers will speak in either for or against the motion. Judges and audience can ask a question related to those topics.(THE DURATION OF QUESTION AND ANSWER FOR EACH TEAM/PARTICIPANT SHOULD NOT EXCEED 2 MINUTES)
3. The participants would be judged on the basis of Language Proficiency, Presentation/Delivery and knowledge of the topic.
4. The decision of the Judges would be last and final
